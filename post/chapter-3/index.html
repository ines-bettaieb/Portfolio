Extract meaningful and semantic keywords using KeyBERT and Streamlit

August 4, 2023

Backgroud

In today’s digital age, information overload is a common challenge. Whether you’re a student, researcher, or professional, sifting through mountains of text to extract the most crucial information can be time-consuming and daunting. This is where tools like KeyBert and Streamlit come into play, offering a user-friendly solution for automatic keyword extraction.

Understanding KeyBert: Your Keyword Wizard

Before we embark on a tutorial to build an interactive Streamlit app, let’s unveil the brilliance of KeyBert. Imagine it as your very own keyword wizard. Amidst the maze of words, KeyBert harnesses the power of the Transformer architecture – a remarkable fusion of BERT and GPT, revolutionizing Natural Language Processing (NLP). This architecture equips KeyBert to effortlessly identify the most crucial keywords and keyphrases within any text. It’s like having an astute reader that carefully examines content and illuminates the essential elements. Say goodbye to the laborious process of manually skimming through paragraphs – KeyBert is your guiding light.

For a deeper dive into the magic of the Transformer architecture and how BERT and GPT collaborate in KeyBert, resources like the Hugging Face Transformers Documentation provide insights into these transformative models.

Now, let’s transition to the simplicity of Streamlit. This technology transforms intricate coding into an intuitive experience. It empowers developers to craft interactive web applications using minimal lines of Python code, making it accessible to everyone, regardless of coding proficiency. The fusion of KeyBert and Streamlit combines the power of streamlined text analysis with the joy of seamless interaction.

Ready to Dive In? Build Your Streamlit App!

Are you ready to take the plunge? This step-by-step guide will walk you through the process of building your own Streamlit app. With this app at your fingertips, you can input text effortlessly and receive a curated list of relevant keywords, all expertly extracted by KeyBERT. This journey empowers you to create something impactful without the complexities of coding intricacies.

This Streamlit application harnesses the power of KeyBERT to distill meaningful keywords from text documents. It’s a refreshing alternative to traditional bag-of-words methods like Count or TF-IDF vectorizers, which can often yield noisy results.
